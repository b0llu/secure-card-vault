What you get

Everything your cards need.
Nothing they don't.

AES-256 Encryption

Every card is encrypted with AES-256-CBC before touching storage. The key lives in your device's Secure Enclave — unreachable by any app or person.

100% Offline

No internet connection required — ever. Card Vault makes zero network requests. There are no servers, no syncing, and no back doors.

Card Scanner

Point your camera at any card and let on-device OCR extract the number, name, expiry, and more — instantly, locally, with nothing sent anywhere.

Biometric Unlock

Use Face ID or fingerprint for instant vault access. Biometric data is handled entirely by your OS — Card Vault never sees it.

Encrypted Backup

Export your entire vault to a password-protected .securevault file encrypted with AES-256. Restore it on any device, any time.

Screenshot Protection

All screenshots are blocked at the OS level — on both Android and iOS. Your vault never appears in screen recordings or app previews.

Security Architecture

Built like a vault.
Because it is one.

Every design decision was made with one question: "What's the worst that can happen?" The answer shaped everything — from how keys are generated to why there's no recovery mechanism.

Hardware-Backed Key Storage

Encryption keys are stored in your device's Secure Enclave (iOS) or Android Keystore — a hardware-isolated zone no app or attacker can extract keys from.

PBKDF2 PIN Hashing

Your PIN is never stored. It's hashed with PBKDF2-SHA256 (10,000 iterations) and compared on unlock. Brute-force attacks face exponential lockout delays.

Zero Network Architecture

Card Vault requests no network permissions. It cannot send data anywhere — not to us, not to third parties. Your firewall will never see it call home.

Fresh IV Per Encryption

A cryptographically random 128-bit IV is generated for every single encryption operation, preventing pattern analysis even if the same data is stored twice.
